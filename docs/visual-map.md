## Target widths

- Desktop: 1440 by 900
- Tablet: 900 by 1024
- Mobile: 390 by 844
- Small mobile: 320 by 700

The implementation reflows at 900px and 640px. Product metadata moves below descriptions on tablet and each product becomes a compact two-column row on phones.
