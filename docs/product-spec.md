# ND SOFT landing page contract

## Purpose

ND SOFT is the independent studio index for four active product efforts. The page must make the AppsResolve move unmistakable while giving each product a concise, accurate destination.

## Audience and journey

The primary visitor already knows ND SOFT or one of its products. They should understand the studio, scan all four products, and reach a live product in one click.

## Required content

- AppsResolve: human-reviewed AI application support for SaaS teams.
- Tranqly: a one-minute daily reflection companion.
- PawProof: pet records, scanning, and reminders.
- TeeLesson: operations software for independent golf coaches.
- A prominent statement that application support has moved to AppsResolve.

## Scope

This is a static, one-page portfolio. It does not add accounts, forms, pricing, testimonials, analytics, or unsupported product claims. TeeLesson remains clearly labeled as in development and has no dead outbound link.

## Acceptance checks

- The page is server rendered, responsive, keyboard navigable, and motion safe.
- All three live product destinations use direct descriptive links.
- The title, canonical, structured data, social metadata, sitemap, robots policy, manifest, and custom not-found page are present.
- No starter copy, starter asset, em dash, or unlicensed third-party asset remains.
